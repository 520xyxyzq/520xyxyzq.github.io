Ziqi Lu

I am a PhD student at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, in the department of Mechanical Engineering. I am a member of the Marine Robotics Group in the Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Lab (CSAIL). My thesis advisor is John Leonard.

My research interests lie in the areas of robot autonomy, machine learning, and computer vision. I am particularly interested in achieving long-term robustness in object-level simultaneous localization and mapping (object SLAM).

I received my M.S. degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of California, Berkeley in 2019 and my B.S. degree in Ocean Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2017.
